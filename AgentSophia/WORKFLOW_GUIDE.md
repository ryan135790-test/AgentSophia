## Recommended Workflow

1. **Sophia generates workflow** (showing preview + next steps)
2. **You click "Preview"** to understand the flow
3. **You click "Edit Messages"** to customize if needed
4. **You click "Test First"** to verify with 5 contacts
5. **Monitor for 1-2 days** to see responses
6. **You click "Execute Now"** to launch to full list
7. **Sophia runs autonomously** - handles all responses, follow-ups, meeting books

## Customization Options (Edit Mode)

**Before executing, you can customize:**
- Message content and tone
- Delay between touches (e.g., wait 2 days before email 2)
- Channel sequence (email first vs. LinkedIn first)
- Add/remove channels
- Contact segmentation rules
- Follow-up logic based on responses
